## How to Install:

### Windows:
1. Extract this ZIP file completely
2. Go to Chrome: `chrome://extensions/`
3. Turn ON "Developer mode" (top right)
4. Click "Load unpacked"
5. **Navigate to and SELECT THIS FOLDER** (AI-Chat-Exporter)
   - The one with manifest.json inside
6. Click "Select Folder"

### Mac:
1. Extract this ZIP file completely
2. Go to Chrome: `chrome://extensions/`
3. Turn ON "Developer mode" (top right)
4. Click "Load unpacked"
5. **Navigate to and SELECT THIS FOLDER** (AI-Chat-Exporter)
   - The one with manifest.json inside
6. Click "Select"
